Join UNCC Gaming Club

Why Join Us?

Becoming a member of UNCC Gaming Club gives you access to:

  • Exclusive Gaming Lab Access: Priority access to our gaming lab with high-end PCs, consoles, and VR equipment
  • Tournament Entry: Participate in club tournaments with prizes and recognition
  • Free Events: Attend all club events including LANs, game nights, and workshops at no cost
  • Competitive Teams: Try out for our collegiate esports teams in League of Legends, Valorant, and more
  • Networking: Connect with fellow gamers, potential teammates, and friends
  • Industry Connections: Access to guest speakers from the gaming industry and career workshops
  • Discounts: Partnerships with local gaming cafes and stores offer member discounts
  • Community: Be part of UNCC's most active gaming community

Membership Requirements

  • Must be a current UNCC student
  • Pay $20 annual membership dues (covers event costs, equipment, and club resources)
  • Commit to respectful behavior and following club guidelines
  • Attend at least 3 events per semester to maintain active status

That's it! No gaming experience required. We welcome players of all skill levels from complete beginners to competitive veterans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be good at games to join?

Not at all! We welcome gamers of all skill levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or a competitive veteran, there's a place for you in our club. Many of our members were new to gaming when they joined.

How much does membership cost?

Annual membership is $20 for the entire academic year. This covers event costs, equipment maintenance, and club resources. Payment can be made at any weekly meeting or online through our club portal.

When and where do you meet?

We have weekly meetings every Wednesday at 7:00 PM in Student Union Room 240. We also host game nights every Friday at 6:00 PM. Check our Events page for the full schedule.

What if I don't have a gaming PC or console?

No problem! Our gaming lab has everything you need including gaming PCs, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Nintendo Switch consoles. Members get priority access to the lab during open hours.

Can I join if I'm a graduate student?

Absolutely! We welcome both undergraduate and graduate students. All current UNCC students are eligible for membership.

Do you only play certain games?

We play a wide variety of games! While we have featured games for tournaments (League, Valorant, Smash Bros, etc.), we're open to playing anything members are interested in. Check out our Game Library to see what we play.

How do I try out for competitive teams?

We hold tryouts at the beginning of each semester for our competitive League of Legends and Valorant teams. Even if you don't make a team, we have practice sessions where you can improve and try out again next semester.

What is the attendance requirement?

To maintain active membership status, we ask that you attend at least 3 club events per semester. This includes meetings, game nights, tournaments, or LANs. This helps us maintain an active, engaged community.

How do I stay updated on club activities?

Once you join, you'll be added to our Discord server where we post all announcements and organize gaming sessions. We also send weekly email updates and post on social media.

Can I bring non-member friends to events?

Yes! Non-members are welcome to attend up to 2 events as guests to see if they like the club before joining. After that, they'll need to become members to continue participating.

What Happens After I Apply?

  1. You'll receive an email confirmation within 24 hours
  2. Our officers will review your application (we accept almost everyone!)
  3. You'll get an approval email with payment instructions
  4. Once payment is received, you'll be added to our Discord and email list
  5. Come to your first meeting and get your member welcome kit!

The whole process usually takes 2-3 days. Welcome to the community!
