Game Library

Our Recommended Games

Check out the games our club plays and recommends! These are categorized by genre and platform to help you find your next favorite game.

MOBA Games

League of Legends

Platform: PC

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Players: 5v5 Online Multiplayer

Genre: MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena)

League of Legends is our most popular competitive game. Choose from over 160 champions and battle it out on Summoner's Rift. We have multiple teams competing in collegiate leagues and regular practice sessions for players of all skill levels.

Why We Love It: Deep strategic gameplay, constant updates, thriving competitive scene, and free to play. Perfect for both casual and competitive players.

Club Activities: Ranked team practices on Tuesday/Thursday, tournament participation, coaching for new players.

Dota 2

Platform: PC

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Players: 5v5 Online Multiplayer

Genre: MOBA

For those who want an even more complex MOBA experience, Dota 2 offers incredible depth and a massive esports scene. Steeper learning curve than LoL but extremely rewarding.

Why We Love It: Completely free to play with no pay-to-win elements, extremely high skill ceiling, The International has the biggest prize pools in esports.

Club Activities: Casual in-house games, watch parties for major tournaments.

Tactical FPS Games

Valorant

Platform: PC

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Players: 5v5 Tactical Shooter

Genre: FPS (First-Person Shooter)

Riot Games' tactical shooter that combines precise gunplay with unique character abilities. One of our fastest-growing competitive games with multiple teams forming.

Why We Love It: Combines CS:GO-style gunplay with hero abilities, free to play, active competitive scene, accessible for new FPS players.

Club Activities: Weekly scrimmages, tournament team, aim training sessions.

Counter-Strike 2

Platform: PC

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Players: 5v5 Tactical Shooter

Genre: FPS

The legendary tactical shooter returns with updated graphics and gameplay. Pure skill-based competitive FPS with no abilities or gimmicks.

Why We Love It: Classic competitive FPS, high skill ceiling, thriving esports scene, free to play.

Club Activities: Casual 10-mans, competitive matchmaking parties.

Fighting Games

Super Smash Bros Ultimate

Platform: Nintendo Switch

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Players: 1-8 players local/online

Genre: Platform Fighter

The ultimate crossover fighting game with 89 characters from gaming's greatest franchises. Our most popular party game and competitive fighter.

Why We Love It: Easy to pick up, hard to master, huge roster, fun for casual and competitive play, perfect party game.

Club Activities: Weekly Friday night tournaments, casual free-for-alls, competitive practice sessions.

Street Fighter 6

Platform: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X

Club Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4/5)

Players: 1v1

Genre: Traditional Fighting Game

The latest entry in the legendary Street Fighter series. Modern controls make it accessible to newcomers while maintaining depth for veterans.

Why We Love It: Accessible to new players, deep mechanics for competitive play, excellent training mode, strong online play.

Club Activities: Weekly casuals, combo workshops, tournament viewing parties.
