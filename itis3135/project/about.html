About UNCC Gaming Club

Our Mission

The UNCC Gaming Club is dedicated to bringing together students who share a passion for video games. We provide a welcoming community where gamers of all skill levels can connect, compete, and have fun. Our goal is to promote gaming culture on campus while fostering friendships, teamwork, and healthy competition.

Club History

Founded in 2018, the UNCC Gaming Club started with just 15 members meeting in a small classroom. Today, we've grown to over 200 active members and have become one of the most active student organizations on campus.

Over the years, we've hosted dozens of tournaments, participated in regional esports competitions, and built a strong gaming community at UNCC. Our members have gone on to compete in collegiate esports leagues and some have even pursued careers in the gaming industry.

Our Achievements

  • 2023: Regional Champion in Collegiate League of Legends Tournament
  • 2022: Hosted UNCC's first major gaming convention with over 500 attendees
  • 2021: Established partnership with local gaming cafes for member discounts
  • 2020: Launched online gaming events during pandemic, maintaining community connection
  • 2019: Won "Most Active Student Organization" award from UNCC Student Government

What We Do

  • Weekly Meetings: Every Wednesday evening for announcements, planning, and casual gaming
  • Game Nights: Friday social events featuring different games each week
  • Tournaments: Competitive events with prizes throughout the semester
  • LANs: Large-scale gaming parties with food and giveaways
  • Community Service: Charity gaming marathons and fundraisers
  • Workshops: Skills sessions on game strategy, content creation, and esports careers
